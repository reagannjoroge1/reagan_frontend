About Us

Moran Pharmacy Limited is a fast growing pharmaceutical distribution company specialised in sales and distribution of medicines and a variety of pharmaceutical products. We leverage our wide knowledge of medical trends, wide network of manufacturers and distributors of medicines and pharmaceutical products to supply our clients with best products. We boast of an adequately skilled and well motivated pool of staff including a a pool of delivery people, administration, front office, sales and marketing teams, who are able to advice our customers on the best products in the market. We provide an unrivalled service to retail pharmacies across Kenya and the East Africa Community at large. Our business operational philosophy is based around delivering a timely and efficient service to retail pharmacy, with a personal touch.
