FACULTY OF COMPUTING AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Dean’s Message

The faculty offers two (2) postgraduate programmes, namely ; MSc in Information Technology and MSc in Computer Science, each with optional specializations and four (4) undergraduate programmes – Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Computer Technology, Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

In response to our customer demand we offer a diploma programme in Information and Communication Technology (ICT), as well as professional courses including  Cisco Academy programme (Level 1-4), the International Certificate of Digital Literacy (ICDL) courses , Huawei ‘s HCIA (Datacom, Security and AI). All our programmes are constantly reviewed to reflect the rapidly changing demands in the ICT industry, and are taught by highly qualified and experienced academic staff that are very committed to academic excellence.

Our alumni are key players in the ICT sector as they offer dependable, sought after solutions in technology integration, enterprise connectivity, software development, research and innovation.

FACULTY OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Dean’s Message

Engineering is the profession in which the knowledge of basic sciences and that earned from engineering studies, experience and practice are applied to develop, and to create methods, machinery and equipment with optimum use of materials and resources for the benefit the humankind.True to the above definition, the Faculty strives to meet the challenges of engineering and along with that aspires to achieve a mutual supportive balance among undergraduate and graduate education, research and service to the community.We offer Bachelor of Science degrees in Telecommunication and Information Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Electronic and Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronic Engineering, Control and Instrumentation, and Physics. So far, there are two departments that host these programmes, namely, the Department of Electrical & Information Engineering and the Department of Mechanical and Manufaturing Engineering.

FACULTY OF MEDIA AND COMMUNICATION

The Faculty of Media and Communication is arguably one of the largest providers of practice-based media education and related subjects in Kenya. We pride ourselves as offering the first and only degree programme in Film Production and Animation. We have some of the best-equipped media and communication labs, including more than 22 high-end I-Mac computers, Multimedia PCs, professional TV cameras and state-of-the-art audio and visual recording, editing and post-production equipment. We have a Radio Station that broadcasts 24 hours and an excellent TV Studio. Our TV station will go on air in the near future. The faculty has two departments: Journalism and Communication and Film and Broadcast Production. Our staff is highly experienced and dedicated. The faculty offers certificate, diploma, degree and masters programmes in Mass Communication, Journalism, Strategic Public Relations, and Applied Communication and in Film Production and Animation. The faculty also offers short courses in animation and motion graphics, video production, graphics design, photography and photojournalism. We therefore provide students with the best possible resources for training in Journalism (Print and Broadcast), Multimedia and Animation and TV and Radio Production. Our Bachelor of Applied Communication (B.A.C) has two unique specializations, namely Corporate Communication and Health and Development Communication.
